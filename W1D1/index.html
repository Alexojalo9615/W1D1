Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredienti

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Istruzioni

    Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, miz toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few minutes in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour ...
